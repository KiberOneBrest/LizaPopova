my page about the bear

Bears (lat. Ursus) are a genus of mammals of the carnivorous order. According to paleontological information, the genus of bears appeared 5-6 million years ago. Its first representative is currently considered to be the bear Ursus minimus, a relatively small animal whose fossil remains were found in France.
